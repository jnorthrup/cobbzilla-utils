void org.cobbzilla.util.string.Base64.OutputStream.write ( int  theByte) throws java.io.IOException

Writes the byte to the output stream after converting to/from Base64 notation.

When encoding, bytes are buffered three at a time before the output stream actually gets a write() call. When decoding, bytes are buffered four at a time.
