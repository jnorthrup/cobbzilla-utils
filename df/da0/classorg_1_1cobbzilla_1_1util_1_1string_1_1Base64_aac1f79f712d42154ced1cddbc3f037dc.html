static final byte [] org.cobbzilla.util.string.Base64.getDecodabet ( int  options)
staticprivate

Returns one of the _SOMETHING_DECODABET byte arrays depending on the options specified.

It's possible, though silly, to specify ORDERED and URL_SAFE in which case one of them will be picked, though there is no guarantee as to which one will be picked.
